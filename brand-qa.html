01 — The Problem

Brand pages can disappear quietly. No one was checking.

A brand page that redirects to a search results page — or simply returns a 404 — looks fine in a spreadsheet. No alert fires. No process existed to systematically verify that brand pages across both banners were actually live and resolving correctly.

The risk: customers land on a search page instead of a curated brand experience. SEO signals are lost. And nobody knows until a buyer or writer happens to click on it.

The tool checks every brand URL, follows all redirects, and flags anything that does not land on the expected brand page destination.

04 — Two Versions

Version 1 found the problem. Version 2 proved accuracy.

Version 1 — May 2025
  • 1,008 SC brands checked — 7 missing
  • 1,005 SS brands checked — 13 missing
  • 20 genuinely missing pages found and reported
  • Initial redirect logic — some edge cases flagged incorrectly
Version 2 — April 2026
  • SC: 356 brands checked — 356 passed, 0 missing
  • SS: 18 flags — all confirmed false positives
  • Edge cases (Frye & Co., B-52) now explained immediately
  • Refined redirect detection — passes clean lists cleanly
